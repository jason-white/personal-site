Blood Incantation
Absolute Elsewhere

Holy. Shit. They did it. They made the perfect Blood Incantation album. Only Blood Incantation can do such a thing. Possibly the most polarizing death metal band to date (are there other polarizing death metal bands?) When they released Timewave Zero (a pure ambient album), there was a definite divide in fans that loved it or hated it. Back in 2023 there was talk about the band recording with members of Hallas at Hansa Tonstudio in Berlin. Then Luminescent Bridge was released, but it seemed this was an unrelated single, one side death metal, the other ambient. The guys were cooking something else. A full length LP was finally announced this year, and I don't think anybody was ready. They blended ambient and psychedelic rock with multiple sub-genres of metal in such a way that no actual fan could be mad about. It features Thorsten Quaesching from Tangerine Dream on synths, and if that isn't enough, Nicklas Malmqvist from Hallas is also on keys. There's a portion of The Message (Tablet II) that will make you feel like you've been immediately transported into a 70s Pink Floyd album before being dropped into some of the best thrash metal to bless your ear holes. This album is a pure masterpiece, and I have a hunch they will push it even further.
